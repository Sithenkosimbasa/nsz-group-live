Privacy Policy

NSZ Group (PTY) LTD

Effective Date: The date you accept this Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

NSZ Group (PTY) LTD ("NSZ Group", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is collected and used properly, lawfully, and transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you:

  • Visit our website
  • Request services or quotations
  • Engage with our ICT, marketing, or construction services
  • Interact with our digital platforms or advertising campaigns

We comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa and applicable data protection laws.

2. Information Officer & PAIA / POPIA Section 51 Manual

In terms of the Promotion of Access to Information Act (PAIA) and Section 51 requirements, NSZ Group maintains a PAIA Manual which outlines:

  • The categories of records held by the company
  • The process to request access to records
  • Contact details of the Information Officer
  • Applicable request procedures and fees

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

3.1 Personal Identification Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company/business name

3.2 Business & Service Information

  • Project requirements
  • Service inquiries (web development, SEO, advertising, construction)
  • Budget and timelines (if provided)

3.3 Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site

3.4 Payment Information

  • Billing details
  • Transaction information

Note: Payment details are processed securely through third-party payment providers. We do not store full card details.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing (POPIA Compliance)

We process personal information in accordance with POPIA principles, including:

  • Consent from the data subject
  • Performance of a contract
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests

5. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide quotations
  • Deliver ICT, marketing, and construction services
  • Manage website hosting and technical support
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Conduct SEO and advertising campaigns
  • Communicate updates, offers, or service-related information
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior
  • Support marketing and advertising campaigns (e.g., Google Ads, Meta Ads)

You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share your information with:

  • Trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, payment processors)
  • Advertising platforms (Google, Meta, etc.) for campaign execution
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required

All third parties are required to handle your data securely and in compliance with applicable laws.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure servers and hosting environments
  • Access control and authentication
  • Data encryption where applicable

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purpose for which it was collected
  • Comply with legal, tax, or regulatory requirements

10. Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach that compromises personal information:

  • We will assess the scope and impact of the breach
  • Notify affected data subjects where required
  • Report the breach to the Information Regulator as mandated by POPIA
  • Take immediate steps to contain and mitigate the breach

11. Your Rights (POPIA)

You have the right to:

  • 🔍 Access your personal information
  • ✏️ Request correction or deletion of your data
  • 🚫 Object to processing of your information
  • ↩️ Withdraw consent where applicable
  • 📢 Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator

To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.
